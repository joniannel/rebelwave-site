## Idea 1: Dark Brutalist Command Center

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism meets Dark Tech Dashboard

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, intentional asymmetry — content blocks offset at angles, not centered
- High contrast gold-on-black with no softening gradients
- Typography as architecture — oversized display text that structures the layout
- Every element feels like it was built to convert, not to impress

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #0A0A0A near-black
- Gold: #D6B95D as the single accent — used sparingly but powerfully
- Off-white: #F0EDE6 for body text
- Dark panels: #111318 for section separation
- Emotional intent: authority, urgency, precision

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric split layouts — text left-heavy, visuals right-offset
- Thick gold border-left accents on key sections
- Full-bleed dark sections broken by sharp diagonal cuts
- No rounded corners anywhere — everything is hard-edged

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) in faded gold behind content
- Horizontal gold rule lines that bleed off-screen
- Monospace counter animations for stats/results

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states reveal gold underlines on nav items
- CTA buttons have a "fill from left" hover animation in gold
- Scroll-triggered entrance animations: fade-up with slight x-offset

**Animation:**
- Staggered text reveals on scroll (each word or line)
- Stats count up when section enters viewport
- Logo heartbeat pulse on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Syne (700/800 weight) — bold, geometric, modern
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean, readable
- Accent labels: Syne Mono for small caps labels

## Idea 2: Dark Kinetic Wave — Signal & Noise

**Design Movement:** Dark Futurism / Signal Processing Aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- The heartbeat/wave motif is the central visual language — everything pulses
- Layered depth: background noise textures, mid-layer content, foreground gold accents
- Motion as meaning — animations reinforce the "signal" metaphor
- Sections feel like they're transmitting data, not just presenting it

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep space black: #080C14
- Electric gold: #D4B84A — slightly warmer, more energetic
- Signal green accent: #2A6E50 — used only for "live" indicators
- Ghost white: rgba(255,255,255,0.85) for text
- Emotional intent: cutting-edge technology, live intelligence, momentum

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with animated wave/signal line running through it
- Content in "transmission blocks" — dark cards with thin gold borders
- Diagonal section transitions using clip-path
- Stats displayed in a terminal/ticker style

**Signature Elements:**
- Animated SVG heartbeat line that runs across the hero
- Glowing gold dots as bullet points / list markers
- "LIVE" indicator badge near the booking section

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Parallax scrolling on hero background
- Cards tilt slightly on hover (3D perspective transform)
- Form fields have a gold focus glow

**Animation:**
- Hero wave line draws itself on load
- Section headings slide in from left with a gold underline that extends
- Numbers increment with a digital ticker effect

**Typography System:**
- Display: Syne (800) — all caps for headings
- Body: Poppins (400) — approachable but modern
- Data/stats: monospace for numbers

## Idea 3: Luxury Dark Editorial

**Design Movement:** High-End Agency Editorial — like a luxury magazine gone digital

**Core Principles:**
- Restraint is power — generous whitespace even on dark backgrounds
- Gold is used like ink, not paint — thin lines, fine details, not fills
- Typography does the heavy lifting — layout is typographic-first
- The page feels expensive, not loud

**Color Philosophy:**
- Charcoal: #191D28 as primary background
- Warm black: #0F1117 for hero/footer
- Champagne gold: #C9A84C — muted, sophisticated
- Warm white: #F5F0E8 for text
- Emotional intent: premium, trustworthy, results-oriented

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Editorial grid: 12-column with deliberate column-spanning
- Large typographic pull quotes that span full width
- Alternating left/right content blocks for case studies
- Thin horizontal rules as section dividers

**Signature Elements:**
- Pull quotes in oversized Syne italic
- Thin gold frame borders on testimonial cards
- Section labels in small-caps tracking-widest

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth, slow hover transitions (300-400ms ease)
- Underline animations on links that draw from center outward
- Booking form with elegant floating labels

**Animation:**
- Fade-in with subtle upward drift (no dramatic slides)
- Parallax on hero background image
- Testimonial carousel with smooth crossfade

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (700 italic) for hero headline
- Subheadings: Syne (600)
- Body: DM Sans (400)

## Selected Approach: **Idea 2 — Dark Kinetic Wave**

The heartbeat/wave motif is already the RebelWave brand identity. This approach makes it the central design language, creating a cohesive experience where the brand name and visual style are inseparable. The signal/transmission metaphor also reinforces the AI-powered positioning. The kinetic energy matches the agency's bold, results-driven personality.
